Administrative Competency Endorsement (ACE)

The Administrative Competency Endorsement (ACE) is sponsored by the Foundation for Community Education (FCE) and is conferred upon community education professionals who serve as district wide Community Education Directors or other positions in community education with district wide responsibilities. If you are a community educator with district-wide responsibilities who is seeking a challenging and rewarding process of professional affirmation and recognition, consider applying for the Administrative Competency Endorsement.
